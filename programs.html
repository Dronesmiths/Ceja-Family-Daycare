Our Childcare Programs

Every child is unique. Our programs are designed to nurture growth at every stage of development.

Infants & Toddlers

We provide a warm, responsive environment where our youngest learners feel safe to explore. We focus on sensory play, motor skill development, and building secure emotional attachments.

  • Sensory Exploration
  • Language Building
  • Social Interaction

Preschool Readiness

Our preschool program introduces structured learning in a fun way. We emphasize early literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving skills to ensure children are ready for kindergarten.

  • Early Literacy & Math
  • Creative Arts & Science
  • Independence Skills

After School Care

A safe space for school-aged children to unwind, complete homework, and engage in extracurricular activities. We offer a balanced mix of study time and active play.

  • Homework Assistance
  • Team Building Games
  • Afternoon Snacks

A Day at Ceja Family Daycare

Consistency helps children feel secure. Our daily routine balances structured learning with free-play time to keep children engaged and happy throughout the day.

  • 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM Arrival & Breakfast
  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM Circle Time & Learning
  • 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM Outdoor Play & Exploration
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Healthy Lunch Service
  • 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM Nap Time / Quiet Activities
  • 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM Afternoon Snack & Creative Play
