About Our Daycare

Providing a second home for Palmdale families for over 14 years.

Welcome to Our Family

Hello! I am Silvia Ceja, the founder of Ceja Family Daycare. For the last 14 years, I have dedicated my life to caring for children and helping them grow in a safe, loving, and educational environment.

What started as a passion for supporting working parents in our Palmdale community has grown into a trusted home-based daycare where children feel like they are part of our family. We believe that every child deserves a space where they can be curious, creative, and confident.
