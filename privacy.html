Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

Information We Collect

When you use our website, specifically our contact form, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to provide about your childcare needs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule tours.
  • Provide information about our childcare services.
  • Improve our website and customer service.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality of the site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@cejadaycare.com.
