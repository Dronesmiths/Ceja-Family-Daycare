Licensing Information

Ceja Family Daycare is a fully licensed and regulated childcare provider.

State Licensing

Our facility is licensed by the **California Department of Social Services, Community Care Licensing Division**. This license ensures that we meet strict health, safety, and educational standards required for operating a family childcare home.

License Number: #197700478

Current Status: **Active**

Safety & Compliance

  • Background Checks: All staff and household members over 18 undergo comprehensive background checks and fingerprinting.
  • Health & Safety Inspections: We undergo regular, unannounced inspections to ensure our home remains a safe environment for your children.
  • CPR & First Aid: Our staff is fully trained and certified in Pediatric CPR and First Aid.
  • Nutrition: We follow state guidelines for providing healthy, balanced meals and snacks.

Verification

Parents have the right to review our licensing file, including any past inspection reports. You can also verify our license status by visiting the California Child Care Licensing website.

Subsidy Programs

Because we are a licensed facility, we are eligible to accept various childcare subsidy programs, including **CCRC** and other government assistance programs. Please contact us to learn more about eligibility.
